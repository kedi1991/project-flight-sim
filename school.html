Instructions

Kedi's flight simulator

  1. Click the "Actual Flight" button to start the game.
    This will display the flight screen as well as instrument displays to guide your flight.
  2. Press the RIGHT-ARROW once to prepare the plane for taxi.
    This will make an automatic anouncement and move the plane to runway and automatically stops at the taxi position
  3. Press and hold the RIGHT-ARROW until the plane speed reads Full thrust for about 3 seconds and release. This will make the craft attain a speed -V1
  4. Press the UP ARROW key to rotate the craft up to a mminimum of -15 degrees for maximum lift. Level the plane by pressing the DOWN ARROW key.
  5. Cruise the craft at the desired altitude for for about 80 seconds (the timer should read appr 110s) and start descend the craft by pressing the DOWN ARROW key Also redice the thrust/speed of the plane to 10 units by pressing the LEFT ARROW key.
  6. Descend gradually to the runway and stop the plane.
  7. A successful stop will display a green message showing a completed mission in green text :)
